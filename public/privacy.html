Assessly Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Assessly (“we”, “the app”) helps users practice interviews and assessments using AI-generated questions. This policy describes what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account information: name and email when you sign up (via email or Google).
  • Practice data: quiz answers, session progress, scores, and interview responses you submit.
  • Usage data: basic technical logs needed to operate and secure the service.

How we use information

  • Authenticate you and save your progress across sessions.
  • Generate questions and evaluate answers using AI services.
  • Show your completed tests and session history in your profile.

Third-party services

We use Supabase for authentication and data storage, and AI providers (e.g. Groq) to generate questions and feedback. These providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

Data retention

We retain your account and practice history while your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting us.
